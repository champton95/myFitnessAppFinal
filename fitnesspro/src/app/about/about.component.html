ABOUT MYFITNESS PRO

MyFitness Pro is an application designed to help any person looking to improve their health and daily lifestyle.

Using the built in Fitness Tracker, a user can log their daily workout routines and track their progression over time.

To use the Fitness Tracker, simply click on one of the preset workouts in the left menu. A prompt will appear asking to log the time of the workout, and then the exercise will be added to the routine list for the day. It's that easy!

Additionally, you can live chat with others and share your routines for new ideas!
